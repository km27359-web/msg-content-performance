Top 3 best send windows

1

Any day, 10-11 AM ET

Mid-morning sends average 45.0% open rate and 13.1% click rate across 8 campaigns. This window catches the second inbox check of the day when people have settled in and are more likely to click through.

2

Thursday, 6-7 AM ET

Photography Spotlight hit 46.2% opens on 2,800 contacts. Early AM works for content-driven newsletters where the subject line alone drives opens, though click rates drop (1.6%).

3

Friday, 7 AM ET (seasonal/relationship)

Happy Holidays achieved 31.9% opens and best newsletter click rate at 9.4%. Friday morning catches people in lighter work mode, more likely to click seasonal content.

Key patterns

Small lists outperform big blasts. GSW emails (250-320 contacts) average 47% opens vs. newsletters (2,200-2,800) at 31%. Segment harder.

Mid-morning beats early AM for clicks. 10-11 AM ET sends average 13.1% click rate vs. 4.4% for pre-7 AM. People open early but click when they have time.

Friday + mid-morning is the sweet spot. Friday mid-AM sends average 45.0% opens and 13.0% clicks. People are winding down for the week and more receptive to content they can act on.
